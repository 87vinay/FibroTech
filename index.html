About Us

Your Trusted Partner Since 1992

Fibro Tech Chemicals is a Proprietorship Based Firm established in 1992, engaged as the foremost Wholesaler, Exporter, and Trader of a comprehensive range of fiberglass and chemical products.

With decades of experience in the industry, we have built a strong reputation for providing high-quality products that meet international standards and cater to diverse industrial needs.

Our extensive product portfolio includes Fiberglass Needle Mat, Fireproof Fiberglass Fabric, Electronic Fiberglass Fabric, White Fiberglass Chopped Strand, Fiberglass Chopped Strand Mat, Woven Fiberglass Roving, White Polyester Film, and many more specialized products.

We pride ourselves on maintaining strict quality control measures, providing customized packaging solutions, and ensuring timely delivery to clients across India and overseas markets.
