About Us

Your Trusted Trading Partner Since 1992

Fibro Tech Chemicals is a Proprietorship Based Firm established in 1992, engaged as the foremost Wholesaler, Exporter, and Trader of a comprehensive range of fiberglass and chemical products for industries worldwide.

With over three decades of trading experience, we have built strong relationships with leading manufacturers and suppliers, enabling us to provide high-quality products that meet international standards at competitive prices.

Our extensive product portfolio includes Fiberglass Needle Mat, Fireproof Fiberglass Fabric, Electronic Fiberglass Fabric, White Fiberglass Chopped Strand, Fiberglass Chopped Strand Mat, Woven Fiberglass Roving, White Polyester Film, and many more specialized industrial materials.

We specialize in bulk trading, export operations, and wholesale distribution, serving manufacturing industries, composite producers, and international markets with reliable supply chain solutions and competitive pricing.
